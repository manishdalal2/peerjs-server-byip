Local Bytes

Private · Fast · Stays on your network

Share files and messages with anyone on your Wi-Fi — without touching the internet. Your data stays between you and the person you're talking to. Always.

🏠
Never leaves your network Data travels directly between devices on the same Wi-Fi. It never touches Google, Amazon, or any cloud server.
✈️
Works without internet Once connected, turn off your router's internet — transfers keep going. You only need the local network.
⚡
Blazing fast transfers No round-trip to a remote server. Data moves at your local network speed — often 100–1000 Mbps.
🔒
End-to-end encrypted Built on WebRTC — every byte is encrypted in transit. Nobody in between can read your data.
👁️‍🗨️
Zero tracking, zero accounts No sign-up, no ads, no telemetry. Open source and self-hosted — you own the server.
📡
Same network only Peers on other networks or VLANs can't see you. Only devices on your Wi-Fi show up.
🌍
You're literally saving the planet Every file sent here skips the data center entirely. Global data centers burn through hundreds of billions of litres of water and hundreds of terawatt-hours of electricity every year — just to store files that sit on servers forever, long after you've forgotten them. Peer-to-peer means your data lives nowhere except the two devices talking to each other. No idle servers. No wasted cooling. No permanent copies. You transfer, it's done. The planet thanks you.
💧 No water wasted ⚡ No idle electricity 🗑️ No server storage ♻️ Zero carbon footprint
